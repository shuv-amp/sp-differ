# Code of Conduct

We are committed to a respectful, inclusive community.

## Expected Behavior

- Be kind and constructive.
- Assume good intent.
- Focus on the work and the evidence.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment or discrimination.
- Personal attacks or threats.
- Sharing private information without consent.

## Reporting

If you experience or witness a violation, please open an issue and mark it as a conduct concern. Private reporting channels will be added once the project is public.
